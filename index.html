FAQ

Good questions

Q. Does it work on all LinkedIn jobs?

JobVisor runs on LinkedIn job pages. Open any job listing by search through search bar on jobs page and the overlay appears automatically with its analysis.

Q. Is my resume safe?

Yes. Your resume is stored locally on your own device, never on our servers. When you run a match, only the text needed for that analysis is sent securely for processing. We never sell or share your data. See our privacy policy.

Q. Is the analysis 100% accurate?

No tool can promise that. JobVisor's scores, sponsorship insights, and recommendations are guidance to help you decide faster — not guarantees. Data comes from public records and automated analysis, which can be incomplete or out of date. Always verify each metric and suggestion yourself before acting on it. See our terms of use for details.

Q. Do I need to pay to use it?

No. The Freemium plan is free forever and includes 10 full job analyses every day — each with H-1B sponsor history, your resume match score, and the international friendliness score. Paid plans (coming soon) simply remove the daily limit.

Q. Where does the H-1B data come from?

From publicly available U.S. Department of Labor and USCIS H-1B petition records. JobVisor surfaces a company's historical sponsorship activity so you can gauge likelihood — it isn't a guarantee of future sponsorship.

Q. Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Paid plans can be cancelled at any time from your billing portal, and you keep access until the end of your billing period.
